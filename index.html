Our Story

Our school began as a family-run vision, founded by educator Randah Alhorani, Head of School, and her daughter Jana Tayfour, Curriculum Manager, starting with just two teachers and three preschool-aged children. From the beginning, we set out to build a private Islamic school that nurtures the whole child through strong early childhood education, faith-based learning, and academic excellence. Today, we serve over 30 students from preschool through primary school, continuing our commitment to individualized learning, close parent partnerships, and a warm, values-driven school community rooted in Islamic principles.

About Jana Academy Elementary School

Jana Academy Elementary School follows the Ontario curriculum and is rooted in Islamic identity, with a strong Arabic language program. Throughout our dedicated educators, and strong partnerships with families and communities, we prepare confident learners and responsible global citizens.

1. Preschool

Children build strong foundations in both English and Arabic through a rich bilingual environment led by an English-speaking educator, an Arabic-speaking educator, and supportive bilingual staff.

2. Kindergarten

Our goal is to help children grow not just as students, but as people. We create a supportive space where learning feels engaging, and where kids are guided to build strong relationships, make sense of their emotions, and show empathy.

3. Elementary Grades

Our students learn in a setting that encourages curiosity by inviting conversation, teamwork, hands-on exploration, and thoughtful engagement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and welcoming environment that fosters a love of science and learning, making school a second home for our pupils. We deliver innovative, high-quality education that promotes academic excellence and preserves Islamic identity through dedicated, exemplary educators. We also strengthen meaningful partnerships between generations in the diaspora and their wider communities, respecting cultural and religious values.

Our Vision

We aspire that Jana Academy becomes an academic and educational edifice elevating our generations onto excellence and leadership, a unique model that educational institutions worldwide would benefit from.

Why Families Choose Jana Academy

Bilingual Education

Jana Academy offers a high-quality bilingual education that helps students achieve strong fluency in both English and Arabic. Our curriculum follows internationally recognized academic standards inspired by Ontario education benchmarks, ensuring academic excellence while preparing students for a global future.

Islamic Values at the Core of Learning

As a values-driven Islamic academy, Jana Academy integrates Islamic principles into everyday learning. We nurture empathy, respect, responsibility, and confidence, helping students grow academically, spiritually, and socially while engaging positively with the wider community.

Strong Communication

Jana Academy is a close-knit school community where parents, teachers, and students work together. With a 7:1 student-to-teacher ratio, our small class sizes allow personalized learning, meaningful relationships, and continuous communication that supports every child's success.

Thoughtful use of AI

Preparing Students for Tomorrow: We view digital and AI literacy as essential life skills, with trained teachers, effective classroom practices, and carefully managed AI use to enhance learning safely.

A Mission-Driven Community

Our teachers are carefully selected for both their professional excellence and their commitment to Islamic values, and our parents share a common dedication to investing in their children's growth. When you join Jana Academy, you become part of a united, purpose-driven community focused on nurturing principled learners.
